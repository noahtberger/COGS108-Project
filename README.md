
# Exploring Economic Influence on Lyrical Sentiment in Popular Music

## Overview
An analysis of whether economic conditions in the United States influence the emotional tone of Billboard Hot 100 lyrics. We collected song lyrics from charting songs using Spotify data, performed sentiment analysis, and compared the results against key economic indicators.

## Course
Completed as part of **COGS 108: Data Science in Practice** at the **University of California, San Diego**.
